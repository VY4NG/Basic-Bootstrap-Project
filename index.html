Two Delicious Dishes


Welcome to my second website! On this website, I will be talking about two of my most enjoyable dishes and my top three restaurants to go enjoy those dishes in the Twin Cities Minnesota area. I will be talking a little about what those dishes are and why I enjoy them. I hope you enjoy this content and don't end up feeling too hungry after this visit.


About Pho




Pho is a delicious dish from Vietnamese cuisine. The broth is made of beef and has a rich aroma. As you take a sip of the broth, you can enjoy a savory taste of beef and seasonings. The rice noodles should be just right, not too soft nor hard, then that first bite is a combination of delicous flavor. It is of no surprise that by this simple explanation of this dish, pho is one of my most enjoyable dishes.

About Sushi




Sushi is a delicious dish from Japanese cuisine. The rice is masterfully prepared with the ingredients. The meat, most commonly seafood, is the perfect combination when paired with soy sauce and wasabi. On top of the deliciousness of each bite, you also get plenty of omega-3 fatty acids from the fish you are consuming, which has health benefits. Now, let's talk about variety. There are many different sushi rolls and sushi combination that will keep you interested in trying more. This is why sushi is one of my most enjoyable dishes.
